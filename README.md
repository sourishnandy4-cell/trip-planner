# 🗺️ TripPlanner: Group Travel & Expense Coordinator

Welcome to **TripPlanner**! 🚀 This is a collaborative web application designed to take the headache out of group trips. Whether you are planning a weekend getaway or an international adventure, this app keeps your itinerary organized and your shared expenses perfectly tracked in real-time.

Built as a collaborative team learning project! 🧠

---

## ✨ Features

### 📅 Live Itinerary Timeline
* **Interactive Schedule:** See your trip day-by-day with structured, beautifully styled event cards.
* **Smart Details:** Keep track of confirmation codes, flight times, locations, and custom activity notes.
* **Visual Icons:** Quick visual cues for transport, food, accommodation, and excursions.

### 💰 Expense Ledger & P2P Bill Splitting
* **Shared Expense Tracking:** Log who paid for dinner, flights, or rental cars instantly.
* **Category Breakdown:** Beautiful visual budget charts separating food, travel, and activities.
* **"Who Owes Whom":** A custom, automated algorithm that instantly calculates the exact peer-to-peer balances so you can settle up with a single tap.

### 📱 Fully Cross-Platform
* Works perfectly across desktops, tablets, and smartphones out of the box thanks to a mobile-first responsive design.

* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS 🎨
